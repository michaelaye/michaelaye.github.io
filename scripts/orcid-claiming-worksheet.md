# ORCID Claiming Worksheet

## How to claim papers on ADS

1. Go to [ui.adsabs.harvard.edu](https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu) and log in
2. Click the **ORCID icon** in the top nav bar → authorize ADS via OAuth
3. Go to [ORCID settings](https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/user/settings/orcid) → fill in your affiliation and **all name variations** (K.-M. Aye, Klaus-Michael Aye, K. M. Aye, KM Aye, etc.)
4. Toggle **ORCID mode** on (ORCID button in nav bar)
5. Search for papers below → check boxes → click **Claim** in the ORCID Bulk Actions sidebar
6. Claims index within ~24 hours → your `orcid:0000-0002-4088-1928` query will pick them up

**Bulk claiming is supported** — select multiple results and claim in one action.

